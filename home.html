Welcome to Oxford Sofa Company


The term "Oxford Sofa" refers to various sofa models offered by
different furniture manufacturers, each with its unique design and features.
This design showcases sleek horizontal lines with generous track arms,
a tight back, and English seams. Cushion-length kidney pillows accentuate
the silhouette, and bun feet with aged brass ferrules and casters add a touch
of traditional style.
